Pokédex

During your adventure, you could encounter the following Pokémon:

Durant - The Iron Ant Pokémon

Type: Bug/Steel

Abilities:
Swarm
Hustle
Truant (HA)

Pokédex Entry:

Durant dig nests in mountains.
They build their complicated, interconnected tunnels into mazes.



Kleavor - The Axe Pokémon

Type: Bug/Rock

Abilities:
Swarm
Sheer Force
Sharpness (HA)

Pokédex Entry:

This Pokémon is a rough, crude, and violent sort.
It swings around its large, heavy stone axes to finish off its prey.



Feraligatr - The Big Jaw Pokémon

Type: Water

Abilities:
Torrent
Sheer Force (HA)

Pokédex Entry:

It usually moves slowly, but it goes at blinding speed when it attacks and bites prey.



Zarude - The Rogue Monkey Pokémon

Type: Dark/Grass

Abilities:
Leaf Guard

Pokédex Entry:

Once the vines on Zarude's body tear off, they become nutrients in the soil.
This helps the plants of the forest grow.



Mew - The New Species Pokémon

Type: Psychic

Abilities:
Synchronize

Pokédex Entry:

It's very intelligent and can use an incredible variety of moves.
Many believe that all other Pokémon are descendants of this one.
